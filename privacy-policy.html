Privacy Policy for Tab Launcher Extension

Last Updated: 27-December-2025

1. Introduction

Tab Launcher is a browser extension that helps users save and automatically restore their browser tabs. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

The Extension collects the following information:

  • Tab URLs: The web addresses of tabs you choose to save
  • Tab Titles: The titles of the saved tabs
  • Tab Order: The sequence in which tabs are arranged
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences for the Extension's behavior

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the Extension's core functionality:

  • To save and restore your selected browser tabs
  • To open saved tabs automatically when you start your browser
  • To maintain your tab organization preferences

4. Data Storage and Location

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. We do not:

  • Send any data to external servers
  • Store your data in the cloud
  • Access or transmit your browsing history beyond the tabs you explicitly save

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not:

  • Sell, trade, or transfer your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertising networks
  • Use your data for analytics or tracking
  • Combine your data with other sources

6. Permissions Used

The Extension requires the following permissions:

  • "tabs" permission: To read tab URLs and titles when you choose to save them
  • "storage" permission: To save your tab data locally on your device

7. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View all saved tabs within the Extension
  • Delete individual saved tabs
  • Clear all saved data through the Extension's interface
  • Uninstall the Extension to remove all associated data

8. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your tab information remains secure and private. We implement Chrome's security best practices for data storage.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and through the Chrome Web Store listing.
